I'm

JUNIOR UX/UI Designer, Developer

I like to tuned into perspectives, turn out design.

I guess one of the more attractive elements of design is the psychology behind how someone navigate themselves through the object, product or app. A good design had been always able to communicate a clear message to solve a problem. For me, I have always looked at how something fits together, the flow, and impact of making change. I would never have thought of design until embarking on a software development bootcamp, where I encountered the terms UX/UI. I found myself focusing on design and overall experience. I understood if a design needed to be explained to its user, it was not serving its purpose. As a way of developing my knowledge, I have continued to design and create new concept within my portfolio that reflects my self-taught journey.
