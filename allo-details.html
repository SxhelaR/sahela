Problem

There is no one single platform for job seekers to manage their job changes.

Solution

To create job hunting platform for life, where users can apply for roles, set their goals and upskill during the course of their careers.

WHAT IS allO?

allO is a job hunting tool that helps job seekers during their career. Whether thats securing their first role, changing careers or simply moving up the career ladder.

DESIGN PROCESS


GOAL

The aim of allO jobs should create a career path for any user, and design their career around them.
